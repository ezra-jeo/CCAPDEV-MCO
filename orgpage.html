LSCS
I joined LSCS with high expectations, hoping to be part of a supportive and dynamic organization that values teamwork and learning. While there were some great experiences, I also encountered several issues that made my time here frustrating. One of the biggest concerns is the lack of clear communication and organization. Events often feel rushed, with last-minute announcements and unclear instructions, making it difficult for members to properly prepare. Leadership could improve by being more transparent about planning processes and expectations. Another issue is the uneven distribution of work. It often feels like the same few people handle most of the responsibilities while others either take credit or are left out of decision-making. This can make it difficult for new members to feel involved, leading to frustration and disengagement. The organization could definitely benefit from a fairer delegation of tasks and better mentorship for newcomers.
